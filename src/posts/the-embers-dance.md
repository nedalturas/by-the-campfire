The stars look down upon the flame,
A fleeting, fragile, dancing game.
The logs turn black, the ash turns white,
We find our comfort in the night.

The shadows stretch across the ground,
Where silence is the only sound.
And in this warm and fading glow,
We let our tired worries go.
