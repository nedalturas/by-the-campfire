"You made a mistake, but that is simply what happens when you are brave enough to make a decision."\
<br>\
Only those who walk through the flames of hardships can cast light upon their shadows. It is a brave thing, to be wrong—to choose the risk of an error over the certainty of doing nothing at all. \
<br>\
To act or to stay still are but two roads to the same end, yet one leaves a footprint while the other only gathers dust. For whether we move or we wither, we are bound to stumble. I would rather fall forward to the unknown than rot in silence where I stand.\
<br>\
A decision made, however flawed, carries a spark that inaction can never kindle.
