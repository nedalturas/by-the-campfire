The woods were deeper than I had anticipated. With every step, the canopy seemed to thicken, blocking out the remaining light of the afternoon sun. I pulled my cloak tighter around me. 

"Is anyone there?" I called out, my voice swallowed by the moss and ancient bark. 

It was then I saw the flickering light in the distance—a campfire. A beacon in the encroaching dark. I hurried my pace, eager for the warmth and perhaps, a story to share.
